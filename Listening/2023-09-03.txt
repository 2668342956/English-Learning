例句1

    原文：I have come here to chew bubblegum and kick ass. And I'm out of bubblegum.
    听写：I have come here to () and kick ass. And ().

    chew v.咀嚼，嚼碎 n.口香糖；咀嚼物
    
    bubblegum n.口香糖

    kick ass
    1、决定性的胜利；非常出色或令人印象深刻；打败某人或将某人打得很惨；
    2、表示幸福或成就感

例句2
    原文：Don't ever let somebody tell you, you can't do something.
    听写：Don't () let somebody tell you, you can't do something.

例句3
    原文：The secret of life is just to live every moment.
    听写：The secret of life is just (to) live [at] every moment.

例句4
    原文：Another rogue trader has cost a bank billions.
    听写：Another () trader has () [the] bank (). 

    rogue n.无赖，流氓；淘气鬼，调皮鬼 adj.暴戾的，行为失常的
    amateur n.业余爱好者；外行 adj.非专业的；不熟练的

例句5 
    原文：She received the Nobel peace price for her work on behalf of the poor.
    翻译：因为她为穷人做的工作，她获得了诺贝尔和平奖。

    On behalf of（等价于 for）：为了，代表

例句6 
    原文：Young women have taken to calling these jobs as "lazy girl jobs".
    翻译：年轻女性习惯于将这些工作称为“懒女孩的工作”。

    take to 养成...的习惯
